About Us

Ubuntu Threads is a South African clothing brand founded in Cape Town. We work with local artisans to design and produce comfortable, modern garments inspired by South African heritage.

Our Values

  • Local production and job creation across South Africa
  • Sustainable materials and ethical supply chain
  • Celebrating South African diversity and design

Where We Operate

Head office: Observatory, Cape Town, Western Cape, South Africa

Shipping: Nationwide (South Africa)
